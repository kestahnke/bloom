OUR MISSION

Mental health issues have unfortunately been stigmatized in society, and all too often, people are either scared to reach out for help or do not know where to begin.

Our goal is to increase exposure to certain resources as well as to help you supplement your own mental health care with our tailored wellness plans.

HOW IT WORKS

  • Visit our ROOTS page to learn where to start with mental health treatment.
  • Visit our BRANCHES page to assess where you are in your mental health journey and generate a custom wellness plan.
  • Visit our LEAVES page to access several different wellness practices and other helpful activities.
